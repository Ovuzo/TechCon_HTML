History

TechCon began in 2012 as a local gathering of passionate developers and engineers. With a focus on innovation, the conference quickly expanded into a global platform where technology leaders and newcomers meet to exchange ideas.

Over the years, TechCon has evolved to include workshops, panels, and exhibitions from the world's leading tech companies. Its reputation as a hub of innovation continues to grow each year.

Mission

TechCon is driven by a singular mission: to foster technological advancement by bringing together visionaries, professionals, and learners in an inclusive and collaborative environment.

We aim to empower individuals and organizations to push boundaries, share knowledge, and build a more connected, innovative future.

Past Speakers

Grace Hopper

Grace Hopper was a pioneering computer scientist and U.S. Navy rear admiral. She was instrumental in developing early programming languages and popularized the idea of machine-independent programming. Her contributions laid the foundation for modern software development.

Linus Torvalds

Best known for creating the Linux kernel, Linus Torvalds revolutionized open-source software. His work has powered everything from smartphones to supercomputers and continues to inspire developers worldwide.
